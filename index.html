About Pocket Words

Pocket Words helps you build and grow your vocabulary by saving interesting words you encounter. Create your personal collection of words with their meanings and examples.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a word to my collection?

You can search for any word in the search tab, then tap the "+" button to add it to your collection.

Can I sync my words across devices?

Yes! Enable Google Drive sync in the settings menu, and your word collection will be backed up to your Google Drive account.

How do I see definitions for my saved words?

Simply tap on any word in your collection to view its detailed meaning, part of speech, and examples.

Will my words be saved if I uninstall the app?

If you've enabled Google Drive sync, your words are safely stored and will be restored when you reinstall. Otherwise, words are stored locally and would be lost upon uninstallation.
